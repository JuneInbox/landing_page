Gundam: The Witch From Mercury
Set in a world where numerous companies have advanced into space and constitute a huge economic zone, the story concerns the Mobile Suits known as "GUND-ARMS" ("Gundams" for short), weapons that were developed out of diverted health technology "GUND" which compensates for physical disabilities caused by living in space.
Main Characters

The XVX-016 Gundam Aerial is the main mobile suit of the 1st Season piloted by the protagonist Suletta Mercury.

Suletta Mercury is the main protagonist of Mobile Suit Gundam the Witch from Mercury.

Miorine Rembran is the deuteragonist of Mobile Suit Gundam the Witch from Mercury.

Prospera Mercury is the main antagonist of Mobile Suit Gundam the Witch from Mercury and the deuteragonist of its prologue.
